Cultural & Extracurricular Activities


The organising committees and student bodies of the institute periodically hold sporting activities to keep students occupied throughout the year. The students also are given the chance to participate in inter-state and national level games along with participating in activities and games being held within the campus. The University emphasises on the all-round character growth of students. Cultural activities are a critical part of personality development. UPES under the aegis of Student Engagement and Experience every year organizes a fresher party and the Annual Cultural Festival – Uurja.









Campus Life & Facilities


Due to the wide range of subjects and courses, UPES is home to a large number of students. This has helped promote a vibrant and engaging campus life. A law student has a lot to learn from an engineer or say a designer. There is a sports ground which encourages participation in most sports such as football, cricket, and volleyball. The labs for technical engineering courses are also great and quite impressive. According to me, the best part of the infrastructure and campus is the hostel. The hostel facilities are quite satisfactory when compared to other residential institutes in the country. You needn’t worry about living facilities here at UPES.
